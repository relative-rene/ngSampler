Earning

Points

How can I earn points?

There are a few possible ways to earn points. Points
are given by: sharing content, connecting social media
accounts, participating in surveys, giving donations,
and inviting other employees to join the network.

Where can I redeem my points?

You can redeem your points in the reawards section of
habitat. The number of points you have determines
the rewards you are able to recieve.

Gerties

What is a Gertie?

A Gertie is a symbol of recognition given by one employee
to another.

How do Gerties work?

A Gertie can be sent from the feed or from below
by clicking on the "Send a Gertie" button. When someone
recieves a Gertie, the Gertie is stored on the Gertie shelf,
and can be viewed on the user's profile page.

What are the limitations?

Each individual is only able to send 5 Gerties a month.
